Safety Policy
  • To ensure that every employee at work is safe and without risk to health.
  • By maintaining safe working condition and environment by by providing protection against hazards.
Occupational health should aim at:
  • the promotion and maintenance of the highest degree of physical, mental and social well-being of workers in all occupations;
  • the prevention amongst workers of departures from health caused by their working conditions;
  • the protection of workers in their employment from risks resulting from factors adverse to health;
  • the placing and maintenance of the worker in an occupational environment adapted to his physiological and psychological capabilities; and, to summarize, the adaptation of work to man and of each man to his job.

Quality Policy

  • Ordnance Clothing Factory, Shahahjahanpur is comitted to supply quality product as per the requirement of customer satisfaction through continual improvement of quality management system.

  • We the employees of OCF Shahahjahanpur are committed to keep our plant and machinery in fully working condition for optimum utilisation with zero defect,zero accident and zero breakdown in achieving highest productivity.
Environnmental Policy
  • We the employees of OCF are comitted to protect our environment and achieve sustainable development through a well documented environmental system in all the spheres of our activities.

    • WE shall also strive to:-
  • Protect the environment and achieve improvement in our environmental performance towards prevention of pollution.
  • Minimise adverse environmental impacts of our activities, products and services wherever feasible.
  • Optimise utilization of natural resources like energy, water and oil etc.
  • Comply with applicable legal and other requirements.
  • Communicate the policy to all persons working for or on behalf of ocfs and make the same available to the public and also to periodically review the objectives and targets.
