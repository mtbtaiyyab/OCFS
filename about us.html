About US
  • Ordnance Clothing Factory Shahjahanpur has a very old historical background.

  • In the year 1879, sir Ashley Eden , Lt. Governor of Bengal founded 3 clothing factory in Bombay , Alipur and Madras for stitching of garments.

  • In the year 1914 , a part of the Alipur Factory was shifted to Shahjahanpur on experimental basis for 1 year and named as ARMY CLOTHING FACTORY SHAHJAHANPUR.

  • In the year 1925 the entire Alipur factory was closed and work was permanently transfered to shahjahanpur . Shahjahanpur is about 180 k.m.from Lucknow and 330 k.m. form New Delhi.

  • The nearby place of intersests are Nanital and Dudva National park.

  • Ordnance Clothing Factory Shahjahanpur is engaged in the production of textile and clothing items for Army ,Navy , Air Force DGBR ,MHA and other civil athorities.

  • The factory is spread in an area 20.02 hectare and estate is spread in an area of 125.1 hectare.
